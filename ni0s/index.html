0. Setup

Like I said, you'll need a jailbroken iDevice. Ni0S prefers small devices.

I used Evasi0n for the current jailbreak (iOS 6.1.2). It takes about 8 minutes on a Mac 2007.(Although it says 5) Download your native jailbreak utility and follow the Jailbreak process.

 

When you are done, open Cydia. Cydia is a Jailbreaker's app store. You con find more about it in the app.

Current Cydia GUI

Go to the 'Manage' tab in the bottom and tap on the middle selection called 'Sources'. Tap on 'Edit' in the top right. Then, tap 'Add' in the top left. A popup will show up.

You would see 'http://' preinputted in the text box. Type in 'sinfuliphonerepo.com' after the 'http://.' The finished type would look like:

http://sinfuliphonerepo.com

Give it some time. A black screen will appear and terminal information will flow. When cydia finishes its things, there will be created a new 'Return to Cydia' button. Tap. You will end up where you left off.

After that, go to the search tab and search for 'ifile.' There will be two types of ifile, like search results of google. The one with the blue text is the legitimate one, where kind people like to spend their money.

As you can see, I use cracked software and that the installed packages show a check mark next to them.

But there will also be a plain black text selection. Tap on that and tap 'Install' in the top right. Cydia will download its stuff and when done, continue by tapping on the button on the bottom. Dont freak out if your phone freezes. When your phone is thawed go to Cydia and search for 'xbackup.' Same thing with ifile, dont download the blue text one. (Unless you want to be kind and pay which is recommended) Lastly, install 'Safari Download Manager.' You will use it to download the ni0s files. Do your thing and then you will have three new apps.
